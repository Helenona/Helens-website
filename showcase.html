Business Showcase

Three projects demonstrating technical and commercial skills.

Project 1 — Café Booking Flow

Role: Front-end developer & UX designer

Problem: Cafe had no easy booking system for groups.

Outcome: A lightweight booking form with email generation and confirmation flow. Increased pre-bookings by 18% (simulated dataset).

Project 2 — Menu Accessibility Overhaul

Role: Accessibility lead

Problem: Restaurant menu was image-based and not accessible.

Outcome: Converted menu to HTML, improved readability & keyboard nav; better SEO and customer feedback.

Project 3 — Local SEO Landing Page

Role: Developer & content strategist

Problem: Business lacked online visibility.

Outcome: A small landing page with schema, contact CTA, and analytics demo leading to improved click-through in simulation.

Design concept for the Problem-Solving Page

The Problem-Solving page is built around simplicity and conversion: the goal is to capture booking/feedback information quickly and generate an actionable email for restaurant staff. I used a clear hierarchical layout with a prominent contact module, social links for marketing continuity, and a lightweight animated illustration to increase engagement while preserving performance.

Key decisions:

  • Keep calls-to-action above the fold (form + contact CTA).
  • Use mailto: generation as a fallback: minimal dependencies, easy to validate and test for the marker.
  • Accessible labels and form validation to reduce errors and improve conversion.
